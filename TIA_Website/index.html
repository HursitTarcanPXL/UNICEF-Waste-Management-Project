ABOUT US

"Poor waste management is a growing concern among South Africans. Tons of waste that could otherwise be recycled are carelessly thrown away on a daily basis, thus leading to drastic over-filling of landfills. The goal of this project is to create an innovative IT solution to help reduce the amount of recyclable goods being needlessly disposed of and thus reduce the capacity of landfills in South Africa all while still finding ways to involve the larger community and assist the already established recycling industry in South Africa. Students from various disciplines in the IT world, such as Software Engineering, Cyber Security, Artificial Intelligence, and System Networking, as well as from across the globe (Belgium, the Netherlands, and South Africa) have banded together to tackle this issue head-on and provide a solution that checks all the boxes! Contributors include: Evaluna van Dongen, Lloyd Hendry, Kyle Opperman, Luke-Juergen Mross, Hursit Tarcan, Robbe Bryssinck, and Robin Milissen. Join us on our mission to help us improve waste management in South Africa! Download our app from the “Download” page."
